Application Upgrade project

I have been involved in six distinct phases of work, during which a total of 37 applications were processed for application upgrade.

Tasks completed as part of the project:

Conducting a thorough analysis of the existing ETL processes and workflows to identify areas that require improvement. Planning and designing the upgraded ETL solution, which involves creating a roadmap for the upgrade process, determining the scope and objectives of the project, and defining the target architecture. Developing and testing the upgraded ETL solution, which may involve creating new ETL workflows, migrating existing workflows, and integrating new features and functionality into the ETL environment. Ensuring that the upgraded ETL solution is fully functional and meets the requirements of the project stakeholders by conducting thorough testing and validation. Developing and implementing a data migration strategy to ensure that all data is successfully transferred from the existing ETL environment to the upgraded environment. Providing training and support to end-users and other stakeholders to ensure that they are able to use the upgraded ETL environment effectively. Performing ongoing maintenance and support activities to ensure that the upgraded ETL environment remains functional and up-to-date with the latest software and hardware requirements.
